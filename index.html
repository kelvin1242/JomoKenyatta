Jomo Kenyatta

Jomo Kenyatta was a Kenyan politician and statesman who served as the first president of Kenya from 1963 to 1978. He played a significant role in the country's struggle for independence from British colonial rule and is remembered as a major figure in African decolonization.

"You and I must work together to develop our country, to get education for our children, to have doctors, to build roads, to improve or provide all day-to-day essentials."
